Electronic waste, electricity consumption and greenhouse gas emissions
Choose a web host that combines economical servers with renewable energy, and which offsets its greenhouse gas emissions. The six criteria to bear in mind are:

1. WEEE (Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment) management
2. Data center energy efficiency
3. Sustainable purchasing policy
4. Corporate social responsibility policy
5. Renewable energy power supplies
6. Carbon offset
