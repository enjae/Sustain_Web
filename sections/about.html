We help you design and build more sustainable digital services

Data centers, transmission networks, and the devices we use to get online all run on electricity, and all contribute to enormous emissions of CO2 into the atmosphere. Your website might be a part of the problem.

Use our calculator to obtain a precise estimation of your website's carbon footprint. Then, use some of the guidelines from Best Practices for designing an eco-friendly website

Evaluate the environmental impact of your online services and create a sustainable website.
Our tool calculates the electricity consumption coming from data traffic, by modelling the electricity consumed by:

  • Data centers through which data transfer
  • Underlying Sea Cables
  • The Page transfer size
We have the ambition to educate people on a green internet usage. It consumes a lot of energy to render webpages. Check your current carbon impact and design more eco-friendly websites to SaveTheInternet. Visualizing it will get you to understand that impacts of digital technologies on climate change and natural resources are not virtual, although they are hidden behind our screens.
